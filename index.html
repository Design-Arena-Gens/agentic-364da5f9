About SFO

San Francisco International Airport is the largest airport in Northern California and the second busiest in California. Serving over 50 million passengers annually, SFO is a world-class gateway to the San Francisco Bay Area.

Located 13 miles south of downtown San Francisco, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional customer service, innovative sustainability initiatives, and a uniquely San Francisco experience.
